DISTRICT 4

Illinois's 4th congressional district is a congressional district in the U.S. state of Illinois. The district includes segments of many neighborhoods within Cook County.

The 4th Congressional District was created after the 1990 Census demonstrated a dramatic growth of the Latino population in the area. Drawing on Section 2 of the Voting Rights Act, communities of interest residing across Chicago’s Northwest and Southwest sides and Cook County suburbs were combined to create the Midwest’s first Latino majority voting rights district. After the 2020 Census, to reflect a growing Latino population, a new Latino influence district was created and the boundaries of IL-04 shifted to include more of Chicago’s Southwest side and Cook County and expand into DuPage County. The district continues to be home to predominantly Latino, immigrant, and working-class communities of all backgrounds.
